Information About App

  • Absentees is an Android application designed to provide an abstract view of a student's personal attendance.
  • Currently, it is available only for Semester 3 students of IIIT SriCity, with plans to update it for all batches in the future.
  • The application allows users to:
    • Maintain a personal record of the number of classes missed.
    • Track the number of classes needed to maintain a safe grade, all within an attractive and interactive UI.
  • A timetable and almanac with a clean and clear design help users plan vacations or trips effectively.
  • Users can:
    • Add extra classes or remove scheduled classes from their dashboard.
    • Undo actions if a mistake occurs.
  • Before resetting your phone or reinstalling the app, note that:
    • There is a risk of losing data during reinstallation, so make sure to save your values and update them afterward.
  • For any inquiries, visit the contact page and fill out the form.
